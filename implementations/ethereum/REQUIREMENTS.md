# Requirements

## Functional requirements planned

- register a proof of evidence;
- associate a projectId;
- associate a stage from 1 to 8;
- register a hash;
- register a URI;
- register an issuer;
- register a timestamp;
- query evidence;
- verify a hash.

## Non-functional requirements planned

- simplicity;
- security;
- privacy;
- reproducibility;
- testability;
- documentation;
- use of testnet;
- absence of personal data.

## Out of scope

- onchain grades;
- onchain names;
- enrollment numbers;
- tokens;
- NFTs;
- payments;
- login;
- database;
- marketplace;
- governance;
- onchain file storage.
