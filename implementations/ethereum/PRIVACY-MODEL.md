# Privacy Model

## Onchain

- projectId not personal;
- stage;
- evidenceHash;
- evidenceURI;
- issuer;
- timestamp.

## Offchain

- name;
- e-mail;
- enrollment number;
- grade;
- feedback;
- documents;
- forms;
- authorizations;
- any sensitive data.

The blockchain proves the existence and integrity of an academic artifact. It does not store the student’s identity, grade, or detailed assessment.

## Risks

- URI correlation;
- accidental exposure of names;
- immutability;
- public repositories;
- wallet association;
- explorer indexing.

## Mitigations

- anonymous IDs;
- URLs without personal data;
- review before registration;
- testnet usage;
- separation between proof and content;
- consent for disclosure;
- data minimization.
